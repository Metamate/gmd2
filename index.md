# Welcome

Welcome to the course website for **GMD2** (Game Development 2). This course dives deep into **software design and architecture applied to games**. You will explore advanced game programming patterns, engine architecture, and design principles, developing the competencies necessary to create robust, scalable, and engaging games.

Use the sidebar to navigate through the course sessions, which include preparation materials and practical exercises.

## Course Structure

The course spans 12 sessions, covering 8 core topics and 3 hands-on projects. Each session typically follows this structure:

1.  **Preparation**: Engage with assigned learning paths and readings before class. We cover most theoretical content here to maximize practical time during sessions.
2.  **Recap**: A focused review of essential concepts to kick off the class.
3.  **Exercises**: Hands-on practice to reinforce and expand your skills.
4.  **Review & Discussion**: Collaborative review of exercises and solutions.

This approach allows you to learn theory at your own pace, making our time together dynamic and interactive. Active preparation and participation are key to your success in this course.
